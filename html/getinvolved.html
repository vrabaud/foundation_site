Helping the OpenCV Library

Whether you are an individual or a corporation, you can help the OpenCV library in many ways as detailed on the Contribute page of the OpenCV library website.

Supporting Member

By becoming a supporting member of the OpenCV foundation, you help financially. Through your financial contribution, the OpenCV foundation supports:

  • events meant to improve computer vision: workshops, brainstorm session, developer sessions
  • prizes to promote research, code or computer vision activities
  • infrastructure (e.g. web servers, build farms, administrative fees ...)
  • development of the OpenCV library

Any contribution can be made through the "DONATE" link on top of the OpenCV website or by purchasing goodies on our Store.

Supporting Corporation

Corporations can also help the OpenCV foundation through contracts and decisions.

Support:

The foundation offers several levels of supports from our team of developers:
  • level 0: $1000 support within 1 work week
  • level 1: $5000 support within 1 work week and dedicated machine on build farm
  • level 2: $10000 support within 3 working days and dedicated machine on build farm
  • level 3: $20000 support within 24h and dedicated machine on build farm
  • level 4: $30000 support within 24h, dedicated machine on build farm and fixes when errors happen on the machine

Sponsorship:

Several levels are also offered for sponsorship:
  • Diamond: $250K. Level 4 support. Can direct OpenCV development/Strategy/priorities. Board position. Able to brainstorm solutions to proprietary problems with the team. Front page logos
  • Platinum: $100K. Level 3 support. Board position, strong influence on priorities real time support as above. Front page logos
  • Gold: $50K. Level 2 support. Advisory board (suggest priorities). Quarterly brainstorm
  • Silver: $25K. Level 1 support. Advisory board. bi-yearly brainstorm logo on workshops
  • Titanium: $10K. Level 0 support. Logo on prize sponsorship
  • Bronze: $5K. Logo on bounties
  • Contributor: $1K. Contributor page
