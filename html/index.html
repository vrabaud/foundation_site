The OpenCV Foundation

The OpenCV Foundation is a registered non-profit organization that seeks to improve computer vision. It is also the legal organism behind the OpenCV library. Our official statement of purpose is:

To advance all aspects of machine and computer vision:
  • develop and improve algorithms
  • educate practitioners and users
  • increase the number of uses and users
  • enable free and/or commercial deployment of computer vision applications in all areas while providing an industrial quality open and free infrastructure to help achieve these goals.

On this site, you will find information about what we do and how you can get involved.

What we do

We believe a good software basis is key to improving computer vision. The OpenCV foundation makes sure the OpenCV library remains close to its users by:

  • managing the GSOC (3 years already ! 2011, 2012, 2013)
  • organizing workshops (like for CVPR 2013)
  • sponsoring students (like)

How we do it

With extreme motivation ! Computer vision is cool !

We also have weekly meetings during which we brainstorm and decide the future os computer vision software. Oh, and if you come to a vision conference, try to grab one of us, we'de be happy to get any help or feedback.
